Inuit Art 2022 Wall Calendar

Produced annually by Dorset Fine Arts, this wall calendar features the work of Inuit printmakers from the community of Kinngait (Cape Dorset) and the cooperative Studios located there. This year's theme of water includes artistic depictions of Inuit legends, Arctic animals, and traditional ways of living.

30.5 x 35.5 cm (12 x 14 in.)
Opens to: 30.5 x 71 cm (12 x 28 in.)
